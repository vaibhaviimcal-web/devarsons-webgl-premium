Application-Based Innovation

Established in 1923, the Devarsons group was established by the founding fathers as an application oriented manufacturer of dyes. The focus since inception was on being a "solution provider" rather than a dye supplier.

Manufacturing was established in 1960-70 with Solvent Yellow 14 and Solvent Red 24, targeting Cosmetic and Petroleum industries. Today, we're ISO 9001, ISO 14001 and OHSAS 18001 accredited.

The emphasis within the organization is on developing new molecules or customizing current known molecules to meet highly specialized applications.
